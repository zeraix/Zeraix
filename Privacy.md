# Zeraix Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 16, 2026  
**Last updated:** July 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Zeraix Team ("Zeraix," "we," "us," or "our") handles personal information when you use the Zeraix open-source desktop application, the Zeraix website, and our optional cloud API services (collectively, the "Services").

Zeraix is designed so that its core desktop features can be used without creating an account or signing in. An account is required only when you voluntarily choose to access account-based features or purchase and use our cloud API services.

- Website: [https://zeraix.com](https://zeraix.com)
- Source code: [https://github.com/zeraix/zeraix](https://github.com/zeraix/zeraix)
- Privacy contact: [fergus@zeraix.com](mailto:fergus@zeraix.com)

## 2. Use Without an Account

If you use Zeraix without signing in and without using our cloud API services, Zeraix does not collect personal information from you.

Depending on the features you choose, the application may store preferences, conversation history, model data, and other information locally on your device. This local information remains under your control and is not transmitted to Zeraix.

Zeraix may allow you to:

- run AI models locally;
- connect your own API keys or third-party AI accounts;
- connect to AI services selected and configured by you; or
- use optional Zeraix cloud API services.

When you connect a third-party service using your own account or API key, your data is transmitted directly or as technically required to that provider at your direction. The provider's privacy policy and terms govern its processing.

## 3. Optional Account and Google Sign-In

You may choose to sign in with Google when an account is needed for optional cloud features. When you do so, we receive the minimum account information needed to create and manage your Zeraix account, which may include:

- your email address;
- a Google account identifier;
- authentication tokens required to maintain a secure session; and
- basic account and security status information.

We do not receive or store your Google password. Google processes information involved in Google Sign-In under its own privacy policy.

We use account information only to authenticate you, maintain your account, provide cloud services you request, protect account security, manage purchases and service access, and respond to support requests.

Creating an account is optional. If you do not want Zeraix to receive your account information, you can continue using features that do not require an account or Zeraix cloud services.

## 4. Purchases and Cloud API Services

If you purchase or use a Zeraix cloud API service, we may process limited information necessary to provide and manage that service, including:

- your account email address and account identifier;
- selected service plan and account entitlements;
- order number, transaction status, amount, and billing status;
- API usage totals required to calculate quotas or charges; and
- limited technical and security information required to prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

Payments may be processed by third-party payment providers. Zeraix does not receive or store complete payment-card numbers, payment passwords, or equivalent payment credentials. Payment providers process payment information under their own privacy policies and legal obligations.

## 5. AI Requests and User Content

Depending on current and future Zeraix features, a cloud API request may contain text prompts, documents, files, images, audio, other user-provided inputs, and generated outputs.

Zeraix processes this content only to perform the request initiated by you. Zeraix does not retain prompts, uploaded files, images, audio, or generated outputs after the request has been completed, except where temporary processing is technically necessary to transmit the request and return the result.

Zeraix does not use your prompts, files, inputs, or generated outputs to train or improve general-purpose AI models.

If Zeraix introduces an optional feature that would use user content for model improvement, cloud synchronization, mobile cloud features, or any materially different purpose, we will provide clear notice before that processing begins and request separate, explicit consent where required. Declining that choice will not authorize Zeraix to use existing user content for that purpose.

Please do not submit personal information, confidential information, trade secrets, or other content that you do not have the right to process.

## 6. AI Service Providers

Zeraix may support multiple AI providers and deployment options, depending on feature availability and user selection. These may include:

- OpenAI;
- Anthropic;
- Google;
- Microsoft Azure OpenAI;
- locally operated or self-hosted models; and
- other compatible providers selected or configured by the user.

When an AI request requires a third-party provider, the content necessary to perform that request is transmitted to the selected provider. We limit the transmission to what is necessary to provide the requested functionality. Each third-party provider processes data under its applicable privacy terms, service terms, and contractual commitments.

Where practical, Zeraix will identify the selected provider in the application or related service documentation so that users can make an informed choice.

## 7. Technical, Usage, and Security Data

When you use our account-based or paid cloud services, our systems may generate limited operational metadata, such as:

- request time and request identifier;
- selected API or model type;
- request, token, character, or usage counts;
- response status and technical error information;
- IP address, application version, general operating-system type, and user agent; and
- security events required to detect abuse, fraud, attacks, or unauthorized access.

This metadata does not include the content of your prompts, uploaded files, or generated outputs. We retain operational metadata only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide billing and account functions, maintain service reliability, investigate security incidents, resolve disputes, and comply with applicable legal obligations. We then delete or anonymize it.

Zeraix does not make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects solely through automated processing of this information.

## 8. How We Use Personal Information

We use personal information only as necessary to:

- authenticate and maintain an account you request;
- provide cloud API services you choose to purchase or use;
- measure usage, manage quotas, and process billing;
- process your AI request and return the requested result;
- provide customer support;
- maintain the security, integrity, availability, and reliability of the Services;
- prevent fraud, abuse, attacks, and unauthorized access; and
- comply with applicable laws and valid legal requests.

We apply data-minimization and purpose-limitation principles. We do not require account information for features that do not depend on a Zeraix account or Zeraix cloud services.

## 9. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, Zeraix relies on one or more of the following:

- performing a service or transaction requested by you;
- your informed consent;
- complying with applicable legal obligations; and
- legitimate interests in providing, securing, and maintaining the Services, preventing fraud, and protecting users, where those interests are not overridden by your rights.

Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw your consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect processing that was lawful before withdrawal, but the relevant optional feature may no longer be available.

## 10. How We Share Information

Zeraix does not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, and we do not use personal information for third-party targeted advertising.

We disclose limited information only where necessary to:

- providers that process payments and transactions;
- providers that supply cloud hosting, storage, security, and operational infrastructure;
- AI providers selected for a request;
- vendors that provide customer support, security, auditing, or professional services under appropriate confidentiality obligations;
- authorities or other parties where disclosure is required by applicable law, a valid legal process, or an urgent need to protect users, the public, or our legal rights and security; or
- a successor in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, financing, or transfer of assets, subject to applicable notice and legal safeguards.

Providers acting on our behalf are expected to process information only for agreed purposes and to apply appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

## 11. Data Retention

Zeraix retains personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy:

- account information is retained while your account remains active;
- transaction and billing records are retained as required for accounting, tax, fraud prevention, refunds, and dispute resolution;
- operational and security metadata is retained only for the period necessary to provide, secure, and account for the Services;
- support communications are retained only for as long as needed to resolve requests and maintain an appropriate support history; and
- AI request content is not retained after the request is completed, as described above.

When information is no longer required and no legal obligation requires continued retention, we delete or anonymize it. Limited information may remain temporarily in encrypted backups until those backups are safely rotated.

## 12. International Processing

Zeraix is a global product. Service providers and technical infrastructure may process information in multiple locations around the world. Where personal information is transferred internationally, we use reasonable contractual, organizational, and technical safeguards required by applicable law.

## 13. Security

We use technical and organizational measures appropriate to the nature and risk of the information we process. These measures may include encryption in transit, secure authentication, access controls, least-privilege access, audit logging, security monitoring, backups, and software updates.

No electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. If a security incident is reasonably likely to affect your privacy rights, we will investigate, take appropriate remedial action, and provide notices required by applicable law.

## 14. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- know whether and how we process your personal information;
- access or obtain a copy of your personal information;
- correct inaccurate or incomplete information;
- request deletion of your account and associated personal information;
- restrict or object to certain processing;
- withdraw consent;
- receive certain information in a portable format; and
- submit a complaint to a competent data-protection authority.

To exercise a privacy right, close your Zeraix account, or request deletion of associated personal information, email [fergus@zeraix.com](mailto:fergus@zeraix.com). Please send the request from the email address associated with your account where possible. We may request additional information solely to verify your identity and protect your account.

After closing your account, you may continue using features that do not require a Zeraix account or Zeraix cloud services.

## 15. Third-Party Services

Zeraix may allow you to connect third-party APIs, AI models, websites, repositories, or other services. Zeraix does not control the privacy or security practices of third parties. Review their privacy policies and terms before sending information to them.

## 16. Children's Privacy

Zeraix cloud services are not directed to children below the minimum age required to consent to online services under applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without legally valid authorization. If you believe a child has provided personal information improperly, contact us so that we can investigate and take appropriate action.

## 17. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when our Services, technology, providers, or legal obligations change. We will publish the current version on the Zeraix website or in the project repository and update the date at the top of this Policy.

If a change materially affects your privacy rights or introduces a materially different use of personal information, we will provide additional notice and request consent where required before the new processing begins.
